  • To highlight the decreased reliance on God

和修 MSG Interpretation

7:19

基甸和跟隨他的一百人，在半夜之初換崗哨的時候來到營旁。他們就吹角，打破手中的瓶；

Gideon and his hundred men got to the edge of the camp at the beginning of the middle watch, just after the sentries had been posted. They blew the trumpets, at the same time smashing the jars they carried. All three companies blew the trumpets and broke the jars. They held the torches in their left hands and the trumpets in their right hands, ready to blow, and shouted, "A sword for God and for Gideon!" They were stationed all around the camp, each man at his post. The whole Midianite camp jumped to its feet. They yelled and fled. When the three hundred blew the trumpets, God aimed each Midianite's sword against his companion, all over the camp. They ran for their lives - to Beth Shittah, toward Zererah, to the border of Abel Meholah near Tabbath.

God didn't tell Gideon what to do with his 300 men; he had to think of a strategy for himself, but God was there to make it work. [1]

7:20

三隊的人都吹角，打破瓶子。他們左手拿著火把，右手拿著吹的角，喊叫：「耶和華和基甸的刀！」

7:21

他們圍著軍營，各人站在自己的地方；全營的人都逃竄，一面喊，一面逃跑。

7:22

三百人就吹角，耶和華使全營的人用刀自相擊殺。全營的人逃往西利拉的伯‧哈示他，一直逃到靠近他巴的亞伯‧米何拉。

In Judges 4:15, God similarly threw the army of commander Sisera into a panic.

7:23

從拿弗他利、亞設和瑪拿西全地來的以色列人被召來，追趕米甸人。

Israelites rallied from Naphtali, from Asher, and from all over Manasseh. They had Midian on the run.

Cross reference to Psalm 83:9-16.

7:24

基甸也派人走遍以法蓮山區，說：「你們下來迎擊米甸人，在他們的前面沿著約旦河把守渡口，直到伯‧巴拉。」 於是以法蓮眾人聚集，沿著約旦河把守渡口，直到伯‧巴拉。

Gideon then sent messengers through all the hill country of Ephraim, urging them, "Come down against Midian! Capture the fords of the Jordan at Beth Barah."

7:25

他們捉住了米甸的兩個領袖，俄立和西伊伯。他們在俄立磐石上殺了俄立，在西伊伯酒池那裏殺了西伊伯。 他們追趕米甸人，把俄立和西伊伯的首級帶到約旦河對岸，到基甸那裏。

So all the men of Ephraim rallied and captured the fords of the Jordan at Beth Barah. They also captured the two Midianite commanders Oreb (Raven) and Zeeb (Wolf). They killed Oreb at Raven Rock; Zeeb they killed at Wolf Winepress. And they pressed the pursuit of Midian. They brought the heads of Oreb and Zeeb to Gideon across the Jordan.

Beheading is an execution method that is extremely humiliating to the cultures at the time. It violates the corpse, and in their understanding, makes it impossible to enter into the afterlife. [2]

The name Oreb carries the idea of "raven", just as the name Zeeb carries the idea of "wolf", or how you would associate the name Draco with "dragon". [3]

8:1

以法蓮人對基甸說：「你去與米甸爭戰，沒有召我們同去，你為甚麼這樣待我們呢？」他們就和基甸激烈地爭吵。

Then the Ephraimites said to Gideon, "Why did you leave us out of this, not calling us when you went to fight Midian?" They were indignant and let him know it.

8:2

基甸對他們說：「我現在所做的怎麼與你們所做的相比呢？以法蓮拾取剩下的葡萄不強過亞比以謝族所摘的葡萄嗎？

But Gideon replied, "What have I done compared to you? Why, even the gleanings of Ephraim are superior to the vintage of Abiezer. God gave you Midian's commanders, Oreb and Zeeb. What have I done compared with you?"

When they heard this, they calmed down and cooled off.

8:3

神已把米甸的兩個領袖俄立和西伊伯交在你們手中；我所做的怎能與你們所做的相比呢？」基甸說了這話，他們對他的怒氣就消了。

8:4

基甸和跟隨他的三百人來到約旦河，渡了過去；他們雖然疲乏，還是追趕。

Gideon and his three hundred arrived at the Jordan and crossed over. They were bone-tired but still pressing the pursuit. He asked the men of Succoth, "Please, give me some loaves of bread for my troops I have with me. They're worn out, and I'm hot on the trail of Zebah and Zalmunna, the Midianite kings."

8:5

基甸對疏割人說：「請你們拿幾塊餅來給跟隨我的百姓，因為他們疲乏了。我正在追擊米甸王西巴和撒慕拿。」

8:6

疏割人的領袖回答說：「西巴和撒慕拿的手掌現在已經在你手裏，因此我們該將餅送給你的軍隊嗎？」

But the leaders in Succoth said, "You're on a wild goose chase; why should we help you on a fool's errand?"

8:7

基甸說：「好吧！耶和華將西巴和撒慕拿交在我手之後，我必用曠野的荊棘和枳條鞭打你們。」

Gideon said, "If you say so. But when God gives me Zebah and Zalmunna, I'll give you a thrashing, whip your bare flesh with desert thorns and thistles!"

8:8

基甸從那裏上到毗努伊勒，對那裏的人也提出同樣的請求；毗努伊勒人給他的答覆跟疏割人的答覆一樣。

He went from there to Peniel and made the same request. The men of Peniel, like the men of Succoth, also refused. Gideon told them, "When I return safe and sound, I'll demolish this tower."

Paniel, from Hebrew Panim meaning face and -el meaning God, combined meaning "face of God" or "God's face".

8:9

他也對毗努伊勒人說：「我平平安安回來的時候，必拆毀這城樓。」

8:10

那時西巴和撒慕拿，以及跟隨他們的軍隊都在加各，約有一萬五千人，是東邊的人全軍所剩下的，因為拿刀戰死的約有十二萬人。

Zebah and Zalmunna were in Karkor with an army of about fifteen companies, all that was left of the fighting force of the easterners-they had lost 120 companies of soldiers.

8:11

基甸從挪巴和約比哈的東邊，從住帳棚人的路上去，趁米甸的軍兵以為安全的時候攻擊他們。

Gideon went up the caravan trail east of Nobah and Jogbehah, found and attacked the undefended camp. Zebah and Zalmunna fled, but he chased and captured the two kings of Midian. The whole camp had panicked.

Unlike 7:19, we aren't told exactly what happened or how Gideon won the day, just that he did win.

8:12

西巴和撒慕拿逃跑；基甸追趕他們，捉住米甸的兩個王西巴和撒慕拿，使他們全軍潰散。

8:13

約阿施的兒子基甸從戰場，沿著希列斯斜坡回來，

Gideon son of Joash returned from the battle by way of the Heres Pass. He captured a young man from Succoth and asked some questions. The young man wrote down the names of the officials and leaders of Succoth, seventy-seven men. Then Gideon went to the men of Succoth and said, "Here are the wild geese, Zebah and Zalmunna, you said I'd never catch. You wouldn't give so much as a scrap of bread to my worn-out men; you taunted us, saying that we were on a fool's errand."

8:14

捉住疏割人的一個少年，查問他。他就為基甸寫下疏割的領袖和長老的名字，共七十七人。

8:15

基甸到了疏割人那裏，說：「你們從前譏笑我說：『西巴和撒慕拿的手掌現在已經在你手裏， 因此我們該將餅送給跟隨你的疲乏的人嗎？』看哪，西巴和撒慕拿在這裏。」

8:16

於是他拿住城內的長老，用曠野的荊棘和枳條責打疏割人。

Then he took the seventy-seven leaders of Succoth and thrashed them with desert thorns and thistles. And he demolished the tower of Peniel and killed the men of the city.

8:17

他又拆了毗努伊勒的城樓，殺了城裏的人。

8:18

基甸對西巴和撒慕拿說：「你們在他泊山所殺的人是甚麼樣子的？」他們說：「他們很像你，個個都有王子的樣子。」

He then addressed Zebah and Zalmunna: "Tell me about the men you killed at Tabor."

"They were men much like you," they said, "each one like a king's son."

8:19

基甸說：「他們都是我的兄弟，我母親的兒子。我指著永生的耶和華起誓，你們若存留他們的性命，我就不殺你們了。」

Gideon said, "They were my brothers, my mother's sons. As God lives, if you had let them live, I would let you live."

8:20

他對他的長子益帖說：「你起來殺他們！」但是這少年害怕，不敢拔刀，因為他還是個少年。

Then he spoke to Jether, his firstborn: "Get up and kill them." But he couldn't do it, couldn't draw his sword. He was afraid - he was still just a boy.

8:21

西巴和撒慕拿說：「你自己起來殺我們吧！因為人如何，力量也如何。」基甸就起來，殺了西巴和撒慕拿，取了他們駱駝頸項上的月牙圈。

Zebah and Zalmunna said, "Do it yourself - if you're man enough!" And Gideon did it. He stepped up and killed Zebah and Zalmunna. Then he took the crescents that hung on the necks of their camels.

8:22

以色列人對基甸說：「你既然救我們脫離米甸的手，願你治理我們，你的兒子孫子也治理我們。」

The Israelites said, "Rule over us, you and your son and your grandson. You have saved us from Midian's tyranny."

8:23

基甸對他們說：「我不治理你們，我的兒子也不治理你們，耶和華會治理你們。」

Gideon said, "I most certainly will not rule over you, nor will my son. God will reign over you."

Q: Which part of what the Israelites said in 8:22 did Gideon disagree with? Which part of 8:22 did Gideon agree with?

8:24

基甸又對他們說：「我有一件事求你們，請你們各人把所奪的耳環給我。」因敵人都戴金耳環，他們是以實瑪利人。

Then Gideon said, "But I do have one request. Give me, each of you, an earring that you took as plunder." Ishmaelites wore gold earrings, and the men all had their pockets full of them.

8:25

以色列人說：「我們情願送給你！」他們就鋪開一件外衣，各人將所奪的耳環丟在上面。

They said, "Of course. They're yours!"

They spread out a blanket and each man threw his plundered earrings on it. The gold earrings that Gideon had asked for weighed about forty-three pounds - and that didn't include the crescents and pendants, the purple robes worn by the Midianite kings, and the ornaments hung around the necks of their camels.

8:26

基甸所要求的金耳環，重一千七百舍客勒金子。此外還有米甸王所戴的月牙圈、耳環，和所穿的紫色衣服，以及駱駝頸項上的鏈子。

8:27

基甸以此造了一個以弗得，設立在他的本城俄弗拉。全以色列就在那裏拜這以弗得行淫，這就成了基甸和他全家的圈套。

Gideon made the gold into a sacred ephod and put it on display in his hometown, Ophrah. All Israel prostituted itself there. Gideon and his family, too, were seduced by it.

An ephod is a sacred garment worn by priests. See Exodus 28:1-14 for the construction of such garment.

8:28

這樣，米甸就被以色列人制伏了，再也不能抬頭。基甸還在的日子，這地太平四十年。

Midian's tyranny was broken by the Israelites; nothing more was heard from them. The land was quiet for forty years in Gideon's time.

8:29

約阿施的兒子耶路巴力回去，住在自己家裏。

Jerub-Baal son of Joash went home and lived in his house. Gideon had seventy sons. He fathered them all - he had a lot of wives! His concubine, the one at Shechem, also bore him a son. He named him Abimelech.

8:30

基甸有七十個親生的兒子，因為他有許多妻子。

8:31

他在示劍的妾也為他生了一個兒子，基甸給他起名叫亞比米勒。

8:32

約阿施的兒子基甸年紀老邁而死，葬在亞比以謝族的俄弗拉，他父親約阿施的墳墓裏。

Gideon son of Joash died at a good old age. He was buried in the tomb of his father Joash at Ophrah of the Abiezrites.
